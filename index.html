Project Summary

From text thread to structured archive.

The work began as a reputation and media-organization project: build a clean official profile, separate professional journalism from travel, hold back sensitive private material, organize image and video libraries, and create focused websites for different search themes. The `.ai` site is the project log and systems layer: what was built, why it was split, and how AI helped turn a raw media thread into usable digital structure.

Media Sorting

Images and videos are grouped into purpose-specific buckets: main profile, music journalism, travel, lifestyle, business, projects, private-hold, and future review.

Reputation Architecture

The strategy avoids clone sites. Each domain gets a distinct role, vocabulary, image set, and search intent so the public profile becomes broader and cleaner.

Operational Memory

Project decisions, site counts, blockers, domain plans, and publishing rules are tracked so future work can continue without losing context.
